The Lore of the Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The basic lore of the world:

The game takes place in the Kingdom of Hyrule, where the protaganist of the game, Link, has to liberate the kingdom from Gannon who has taken it over. He will go on many adventures which range from killing monsters, rescueing others, and silly side quests. There are also 4 Divine Beasts which are powered by the champions to help slay Gannon. They're powered by Mipha, Daruk, Urbosa, and Revali.

The character lore:

  • Zelda: Princess of Hyrule, is meant to seal Gannon away
  • Link: The hero of Hryule, meant to assist Zelda in slaying Gannon
  • Mipha: The Zora princess, pilot of Divine Beat Vah Ruta
  • Daruk: The leader of the Gorons, pilot of Vah Rudania
  • Urbosa: The leader of the Garudo, pilot of Vah Naboris
  • Revali: The leader of the Rito Clan, pilot of Vah Medoh
