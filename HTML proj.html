Why I love Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

By: Grace Russell


The game's graphics are beautiful

  1. The shaders that are used are very high quality and are very detailed.
  2. The landscapes are detailed and vast.
  3. The character and monster models are well designed.

The story is amazing and very well written

  1. The story is unique to the games coming before it while still sticking with the original games' tropes.
  2. Each character has a distinct, loveable, and identifiable personality.
  3. The story is well-balanced with saddening story lines and fun tropes.
