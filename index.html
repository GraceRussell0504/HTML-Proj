Breaking down the video game: The Legend of Zelda, Breath of the Wild

The main characters in the game:

  • Link - Main character
  • Zelda - Main side character/love interest
  • Mipha - Sub side character/love interest
  • Deearuk - Sub side character
  • Urbosa - Sub side character
  • Revali - Sub side character/rival
  • Gannon - Main enemy

    • The basic plot points of the game:

  • Hyrule is attacked and taken over by Gannon
  • Link gets badly wounded and has to be put to sleep for a hundred years
  • Link comes back a hundred years later and has to liberate Hyrule
  • Link ends up slaying Gannon and saving Hyrule!
